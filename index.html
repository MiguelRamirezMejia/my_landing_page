When a person, a business, enterprise, or whatever you want to call it, that offers a service, has a web page, it raises its prestige, while allowing customers to increase their level of confidence in the product or service they offer. A website helps increase sales, productivity and market value of any business, company or enterprise.

How long does the construction of a landing page, website and online store take?

The creation of a landing page, web page or online store is determined according to its complexity, its functionalities and its visual characteristics. That said, we can say that a simple project can take a maximum of 1 week to be 100% finished and functional, one of medium complexity between 1 to 2 weeks and one of high complexity between 3 and 6 weeks, it should be noted that this is an approximate, the time can vary according to the goals of the project.
